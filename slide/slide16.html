Future Work
Directions for Improving End-to-End ABSA
Joint ATE + ASC Modeling
Train both tasks simultaneously with shared parameters to reduce error propagation between stages.
High Impact
Larger Pre-trained Models
DeBERTa-large, RoBERTa-large for richer representations. Our results show DeBERTa-base already gains +10 pp on cross-domain ATE.
High Impact
Data Augmentation
Generate synthetic cross-domain training data to bridge the domain gap. Back-translation and paraphrase generation.
Medium Impact
Few-Shot Domain Adaptation
Adapt to new domains with minimal labeled data. Meta-learning or prompt-tuning approaches for rapid domain transfer.
Medium Impact
Implicit Aspect Detection
Handle aspects not explicitly mentioned: "The restaurant was not bad" implies food/service. Requires reasoning beyond token spans.
Exploratory
LLM-Based ABSA
Compare with GPT-4 / Claude zero-shot and few-shot performance. Can LLMs replace the two-stage pipeline entirely?
